Engineering Projects

Recirculating Water Bath (Capstone)

Developed a precision water bath for biomedical testing with ±0.1°C tolerance. Used CFD to ensure thermal uniformity and fabricated 80–90% of parts in-house using stainless steel, plumbing, and control components. Integrated PI control and refined inlet/outlet geometry through simulation.

Mold Design & Manufacturing

Designed and machined a pedal mold using 3-axis CNC, optimizing spindle speed, toolpath, and feed rates via Mastercam and Vericut. Focused on manufacturability, surface quality, and geometry complexity.

CFD/FEA Bike Frame Optimization

Optimized a road bike frame through iterative CFD analysis, reducing aerodynamic drag by 30%. Applied FEA throughout to validate strength and stiffness under dynamic cycling loads.

Cost Analysis of CO₂ Pipelines

Conducted thermodynamic and economic analyses of post-capture CO₂ pipelines. Merged exergy efficiency insights with techno-economic cost evaluations for pipeline design and integration.

Heat Transfer Coefficient Test Rig

Designed a test rig for Accent Refrigeration to evaluate copper coil heat exchangers. Included a 1 HP pump, 40 kW heater, sensors, P&IDs, and CAD models with testing protocols and documentation.

Energy Efficient Grocery Store HVAC

Modeled HVAC layouts with geothermal integration. Used eQUEST and psychrometrics to optimize load balance, equipment sizing, and minimize infiltration in a commercial setting.

Hybrid Tilt-Rotor VTOL Aircraft

Designed a hybrid-electric tilt-rotor VTOL for BC Wildfire Service to carry 10 passengers. Balanced structural design and power-to-weight ratios while maintaining transition flight stability and range optimization.
